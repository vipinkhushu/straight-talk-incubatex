The Era of Digital Transformationclose

21st Century Enterprise (21CE) thrives by creating “United” Experience for its customers, employees, and partners in world which is driven by rapid changes in technology, business processes, and business models. In today’s Digital era, where both consumer behavior and market dynamics are in a state of flux, a 21CE needs to have an outcome based strategy with an integrated focus on Experience and Orchestration. Panel discussion by Key IT practitioners on "The Era of Digital Transformation". Panelists include

  • Rangarajan Kalyanasundaram, Vice President & Head of Marketing, Du
  • Jonathan Richards, Group Digital Strategy Director, Gulf News Media
  • Jambu Palaniappan, Regional General Manager, Eastern Europe, Middle East & Africa, UBER
  • Bryan Carroll, Head, IT Solution Design, National Bank of Abu Dhabi (NBAD).

Manchester United: The Big Leap Forwardclose

The Big Leap Forward: The 21st Century Enterprise Global Summit is a technology summit bringing together G2000 CXOs from Europe, Australia, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and South Africa to build solutions to 21st century business problems. The summit will include sessions and group discussions on next-gen technologies, including digitalization and automation, by business and technology leaders. CXOs from a variety of industries will provide an array of approaches to business transformation. Among the presenters will be Henrik Amsinck, CIO, Lego Group. Henrik will describe how Lego is leveraging digitalization within four “vectors,” in order to transform the full value chain
